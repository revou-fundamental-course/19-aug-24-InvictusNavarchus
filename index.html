About

Horizon Academy is a premier private institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth in students from kindergarten through 12th grade. Established in 1995, our school provides a supportive and dynamic learning environment where students can thrive both academically and personally. We emphasize a balanced education, combining rigorous academics with a rich array of extracurricular activities.

Programs

Early Childhood Education (Pre-K & Kindergarten)

Our nurturing environment fosters social, emotional, and cognitive growth in our youngest learners.

Elementary School (Grades 1-5)

Hands-on learning experiences and project-based activities spark curiosity and creativity in our elementary students.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our middle school program emphasizes critical thinking, collaboration, and problem-solving skills to prepare students for success.

High School (Grades 9-12)

Our high school program offers a rigorous and well-rounded education, with a focus on college and career readiness.
